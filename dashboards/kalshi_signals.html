How net edge is read

For a binary contract, the best YES and NO bids should sum near 100¢. Net edge subtracts the configured two-leg fee and a liquidity haircut from any observed gap.

Interpretation

A flagged market fails at least one execution test. A clean market only passes the screen; it is not a recommendation and still requires independent review.
